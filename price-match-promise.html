Price Match Promise

Travelocompare is one of the best travel companies in the world that caters to travelers offering them budget-friendly deals. We always focus on creating the best travel deals for our customers; hence, we offer a Price Match Promise.

Price Match Promise is an offering by Travelocompare in which if any customer finds a lower advertised price on another website for the same trip they booked with us, we’ll match that price.

Here are a few considerations under Price Match Promise:

  • It applies on an identical trip, including the same hotel, room type, travel dates, itinerary, equal number of travelers, same inclusions, like meals, taxes, etc., and identical cancellation policy.
  • We will match with the lowest advertised price you find only when it is publicly available on another reputable OTA’s website like Expedia.com,Priceline.com
  • If you want to claim the lowest advertised price, you must notify us within 24 hours of booking.
  • You will have to provide relevant links to the competing offer and the necessary documents requested by our Customer Support Team to proceed further.
  • Once the lowest advertised price is completely verified, we’ll offer you either a credit for the difference in price or a full cancellation and rebooking at the advertised lower rate as per our Price Match Promise regulations.
